Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is edresearch?

    EDResearch is an edtech company that provides teaching and learning platforms which helps both teacher and student to teach and learn respectively.

  • How edresearch is different of other edtech learning platforms?

    EDResearch believes in research! Thats how we are different. We provide a wide range of text books in a customized format that makes both teaching and monitoring more organized. And student monitoring is done using state-of-the-art data analysis techniques which have been developed using cutting edge research. Moreover we focus on both secondary and higher education.

  • Who can use EDResearch tools?

    Our tools are for anybody who wishes to teach or learn. We offer our platform for schools, colleges and universities.

  • Is EDResearch free for all?

    Basic plans are free for all. For institutes we charge vey little if they seek certain services from us. However we wish we can make the platform free for everybody someday.

  • I am facing some problems in using the platform. Who should I contact?

    Please send us an email in the address provided in the contact section. We will get back to you.

  • Are there teachers in EDResearch who can teach in person ?

    No, we do not provide teachers. We just provide a platform using which anybody can teach.
